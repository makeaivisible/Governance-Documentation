# Consent and Assent Draft

Draft for legal and IRB review.

Make AI Visible studies how young people interact with AI systems. If you choose to contribute, you may upload an export of an AI conversation. The project will try to remove personal details before any review or analysis.

## Plain-Language Commitments

- Participation should be voluntary.
- Contributors should understand what they are sharing.
- Raw conversations should not be published.
- Public findings should be aggregate and privacy-protected.
- Contributors should be told what the project cannot guarantee.

## Needs Review

This document is not final consent language. It requires qualified legal, ethics, and youth research review before use.
