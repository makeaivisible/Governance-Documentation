# Code of Conduct

Make AI Visible is a youth-centered research project. Contributors are expected to act with care, respect, and humility.

## Expectations

- Protect the privacy and dignity of contributors.
- Avoid speculation about identifiable people, schools, families, or communities.
- Welcome careful questions and critique.
- Keep disagreements focused on evidence, methods, and project goals.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discriminatory language.
- Sharing private or identifying data.
- Pressuring minors or contributors to disclose more than they intended.
- Treating draft research outputs as validated claims.
